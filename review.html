Section 5 — How It Works

How It Works

Three steps displayed side by side with gold connecting lines. Small icons above each step title.

Step 1
Consult
Connect with our wardrobe specialists. Share your project brief, references, and timeline. We'll pull from over 2,400 pieces spanning vintage, designer, contemporary, and western wear.
Step 2
Select
Browse curated options prepared to your exact spec. In-person or virtual fitting sessions available. Expert alterations and steaming are always included.
Step 3
Deliver
White-glove delivery to set, studio, or tour venue. A dedicated account specialist remains your single point of contact through production wrap and return.
