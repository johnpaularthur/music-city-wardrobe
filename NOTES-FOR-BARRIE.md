# Music City Wardrobe — Change Log & Sign-Off Notes

A running record of decisions made on the website, kept separate from the
content review (`review.html`). Bring this to Barrie for review and sign-off.

_Last updated: 2026-07-07_

---

## Business context

- **Music City Wardrobe** is a spin-off site focused on **one aspect of the
  business — wardrobe**.
- It is the **wardrobe division of Fables by Barrie**, which remains in
  operation. Same owner, same track record — this is a rebrand/focus split, not
  a new company.
- Because it's the same business, the two-decade client history legitimately
  belongs to Barrie. The site now bridges the two names so this reads as
  continuous rather than borrowed.

---

## Changes made

1. **Renamed** the site/company from "Nashville Wardrobe Services" to
   **Music City Wardrobe** across all pages. Domain/email updated to
   `musiccitywardrobe.com`. Genuine location references (Nashville, TN) kept.
2. **Trusted-by / credits list** replaced with the confirmed companies:
   Sony Music Nashville · Universal Music Group · MCA Records · Hallmark Media ·
   Mercury Nashville · NBC Universal · RCA Records · Apple TV.
3. **Relabeled** the credits strip from "Trusted By" → **"Selected Credits"** —
   more accurate for past productions; states "we did the work" without implying
   an ongoing endorsement relationship.
4. **Bridged to the parent brand:**
   - About section: "Music City Wardrobe is the wardrobe division of
     Fables by Barrie, dressing the entertainment industry's most recognized
     productions for over two decades…"
   - Footer: a subtle "A division of Fables by Barrie" line.
5. Set up a **git repository** and pushed to a **private** GitHub repo
   (`johnpaularthur/music-city-wardrobe`) so all changes are version-tracked.
6. **Disabled the brand-theme selector** and locked the site to the original
   "House Gold" look (client preference).
7. **Added real photos** to the Contemporary and Western Wear collection cards
   (the other cards still use placeholders).
